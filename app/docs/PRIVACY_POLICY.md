# Privacy Policy for Remembra

**Last Updated: February 7, 2026**

## Introduction

Remembra ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, username, and profile picture (if you choose to add one)
- **Learning Content**: Memory items, categories, and study materials you create
- **Review History**: Your performance data and review schedules

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Features you use, time spent studying, review completion rates
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, app version
- **Authentication Data**: Login timestamps and authentication method used

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve the Remembra learning experience
- Sync your data across devices
- Generate AI-powered study tools and recommendations
- Track your learning progress and streaks
- Send you important service updates

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using Supabase (ISO 27001 certified)
- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
- We implement Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure you can only access your own data
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Supabase
- Authentication and database hosting
- Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Google OAuth
- Optional sign-in method
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Groq AI
- AI-powered study tools (summaries, quizzes, flowcharts)
- Your study content may be sent to Groq for processing
- Data is not used to train AI models
- Privacy Policy: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out** of AI-powered features

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@remembra.app

## Data Retention

- Active account data is retained as long as your account is active
- Deleted accounts and their data are permanently removed within 30 days
- Backup data is retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

## Children's Privacy

Remembra is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.
